RODEO

Travis Scott - RODEO

Ratings

Originality (17/20): A fresh take on trap, blending psychedelic elements with Houston's roots.

Production Quality (19/20): Stellar beats by Scott and co-producers are the album's backbone.

Lyrics and Messaging (12/20): Lyrical content leans heavily on party themes, lacking depth.

Enjoyment Factor (18/20): Addictive for fans of trap's vibe, though inconsistent pacing may tire some.

Consistency (15/20): Strong front-to-back but drags in spots, especially toward the end.

Overall Rating (81/100): 8.1/10
